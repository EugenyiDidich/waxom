our culture

We are a North American communications agency with global reach. We blend big agency thinking and experience with a creative strategic approach to all of our entertainment, arts, culture and lifestyle clients. We plan for success.

Our goal is to function as a dedicated extension of your team. We develop creative custom campaigns and activations, through regional, national and international reach. We have long-standing, trusted relationships with media, that span across the globe.

TARO PR believes in each one of our clients and tell their stories with great passion and excitement.

We are knowledgeable and well equipped to reach a variety of targeted and niche audiences.

We are multilingual with team members fluent in English, French, Spanish, Russian and Japanese.
